---
title: Cost of Living Policies
description: >
  Reporting on government decisions that affect daily living costs,
  including energy prices, social benefits and austerity measures. This
  category links economic choices to quality of life and explores who pays
  the price for political decisions.
---

Economic policy is not abstract. It determines whether people can heat
their homes, afford food or access public services. Articles here break
down complex policy into real impacts and hold decision makers to account.
