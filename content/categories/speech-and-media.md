---
title: Speech & Media
description: >
  Analysing regulation of speech in media, broadcasting standards, press
  freedom and the interplay between news, social platforms and public
  discourse. This category asks who controls narratives and how that
  shapes democracy.
---

Speech regulation does not begin and end with social networks. Broadcast
codes, press regulation and platform policies all influence what people
hear and say. Articles here examine how these overlapping regimes impact
public debate.
