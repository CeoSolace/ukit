---
title: Censorship & Expression
description: >
  Analysis of laws and decisions that impact the freedom of expression, from
  content removal and platform moderation to government‑defined "legal but
  harmful" categories. This category examines who decides what can be said
  and how those choices affect democracy.
---

Understanding how speech is restricted, moderated or amplified is central to
any discussion about democratic society. Articles in this category look at
government policy, private‑platform rules and cultural pressures that
collectively shape what can be expressed in the UK.
